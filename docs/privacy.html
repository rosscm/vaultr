Privacy Policy

This policy explains the limited information Vaultr processes to deliver chase alerts, build Weekly Shelf discovery, support collectors, and keep the service reliable.

Last updated: July 4, 2026

Overview

Vaultr provides Discord-native collector software for tracking specific card chases, receiving private sightings, and browsing prepared discovery shelves.

Information we process

To operate the service, Vaultr may process limited information such as:

  • Discord account, guild, and channel identifiers needed to run the product
  • Saved chases, plan state, alert settings, and source preferences
  • Feedback actions such as More Like, Not For Me, tune-outs, and related discovery state
  • Prepared Weekly Shelf rows, taste profile memory, and related ranking context
  • Operational logs, job state, and backup metadata used to keep the service healthy

How information is used

  • To deliver configured sightings and Weekly Shelf drops in Discord
  • To store and apply your Vault preferences and discovery feedback
  • To maintain service reliability, freshness, abuse prevention, and diagnostics
  • To administer access, plans, and future billing support

Third-party services

Vaultr relies on third-party services including Discord, eBay, supported shop sources, hosting providers, and future payment providers. Information may be processed by those providers as required to operate the service.

Data retention

Vault data, feedback history, prepared shelves, and diagnostic logs may be retained for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the service, maintain reliability, resolve issues, and meet legal obligations.

Applicable law

This policy is intended to be interpreted in accordance with the laws of Canada, unless otherwise required by applicable local privacy or consumer protection law.
